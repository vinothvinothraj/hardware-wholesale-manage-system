# Hardware Shop App Guide

This guide explains the current live app in simple English.
It shows what the app already has and how to use it step by step.

## 1. What the app already has

- Products
- Sales
- Purchases
- Reports
- Inventory
- Ledger
- Customers and suppliers
- Dashboard and sidebar navigation

## 2. Main features

### Products

- Build hardware stock from category to brand variant
- Add price, barcode, and stock details
- Organize paints, construction items, wood, plumbing, and electrical goods

### Sales

- Create sales invoices
- Select customer
- Add items
- Apply discount
- Track paid amount and due amount

### Purchases

- Create purchase orders
- Select supplier
- Receive items
- Track supplier balance

### Reports

- View profit, charts, tables, and date filters
- Download the report as PDF

### Inventory

- See current stock
- See low stock items
- Check reorder level
- Make stock adjustments

### Ledger

- Track customer due
- Track supplier due
- See balance in one place

## 5. Simple use summary

1. Add products first.
2. Buy stock from suppliers.
3. Sell items to customers.
4. Check inventory and dues.
5. Open reports to see profit.
6. Download PDF when needed.
