## 📋 Glossary

### Brand Variant
The specific product offered (e.g., "Philips 9W LED Bulb"). Includes pricing and stock tracking.

### CRUD
Create, Read, Update, Delete operations on entities.

### Ledger
Financial record showing outstanding balances for customers and suppliers.

### Line Item
A single product entry in an invoice (purchase or sales).

### Outstanding Balance
Money owed by customer or to supplier (Total - Paid).

### Reorder Level
Minimum stock quantity - triggers "low stock" alert when reached.

### Storage (localStorage)
Browser-based data storage (automatic, works offline).

### Barcode
Unique identifier for tracking products (optional field).

### Discount
Price reduction applied to sale items (fixed amount or percentage).
