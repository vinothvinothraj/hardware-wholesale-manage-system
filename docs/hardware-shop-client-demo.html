  • Profit cards and charts

Products

Hardware item structure.
  • Create category to brand variant
  • Add stock, price, barcode, reorder level
  • Good for paints, cement, wood, fittings, tools
  • Browse the product tree
flowchart TD
  A([Start]) --> B[Category]
  B --> C[Subcategory]
  C --> D[Product Type]
  D --> E[Model]
  E --> F[Brand Variant]
  F --> G{Stock ready?}
  G -->|Yes| H[/Ready for sale/]
  G -->|No| I[Buy more stock]
  H --> J([End])
  I --> J

Purchases

Buy goods from suppliers.
  • Create purchase order
  • Select supplier and items
  • Record paid or due amount
  • Increase stock and supplier balance

  A([Start]) --> B[Supplier]

  D --> E{Paid fully?}
  E -->|Yes| F[/Bill closed/]
  E -->|No| G[Keep supplier due]
  F --> H[Inventory up]
  G --> H
  H --> I([End])
